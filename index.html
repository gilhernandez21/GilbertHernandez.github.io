Key Concepts I try to follow when creating Data Analysis tools:

  • A dashboard being user friendly and usable is more important than it being flashy and complex to the point where no one else knows how to use.
  • Inputting accurate data results in meaningful outputs. ("trash in/trash out")
  • Try to start with a more general approach then get more specific as you go. A good way to think of it is a mechanic wants to know the make of a car before he looks for specific components.
  • Is your tool answering a question or telling a story? This is usually an important indicator I use to determine whether something is worthwhile or not.
